开机弹出文件夹 Search Enhancement Pack

HKEY\_LOCAL\_MACHINE\SOFTWARE\Microsoft\Windows\CurrentVersion\Uninstall\{F0E12BBA-AD66-4022-A453-A1C8A0C4D570}

执行命令:

MsiExec.exe /X{F0E12BBA-AD66-4022-A453-A1C8A0C4D570}
